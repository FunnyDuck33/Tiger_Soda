Magic Mail
630,000 views
2,419 participants involved
task
To tell the media about the release of a special episode of Harry Potter on Amediateka streaming service, dedicated to the 20th anniversary of the release of the first film about the boy wizard.
ideas and solutions
Who didn’t dream of getting a letter from Hogwarts? And what about a parcel, no less? According to the storyline of the viral promo video, which we filmed for the release of a special episode, the Arctic owl Hedwig delivers gift sets right to the doorstep — to the homes, where magic is expected. In the boxes we put Harry Potter branded souvenirs and promo codes for the annual
subscription to Amediateka. Popular life-style bloggers joined the campaign. They filmed the process of unpacking in Reels format, which became a logical follow-up to the promo video and attracted the attention of the audience.

Project coverage on social media: 630,000 views. Number of participants involved: 2,419

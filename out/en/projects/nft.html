task
Come up with a fun and touching special project for Valentine’s Day, which would show that Citymobil values its clients.
ideas and solutions
We designed a special project as a platform for love confessions, and for this purpose on a separate landing page we set up a digital tool to create Valentine’s Day cards. Everyone could create their own valentines and upload it to a gallery of romantic confessions. First of all, it’s fun. Secondly, this UGC mechanic works well to engage the user in a conversation with the brand.

The participants took part in a raffle for NFT-art from famous artists: Ellen Sheidlin, Darya Fedorova and Vladimir Vikhrov. Users were able to feel like the new Tretyakovs and Morozovs (Russian art
collectors) while the project became more outstanding and high-profile media-wise. So much so that even Paris Hilton liked an Instagram post about the project.

Just three days after the launch, there were 50,000 Valentine’s cards in the gallery! 40% of users who have left romantic confessions, have activated promotional codes for VK Combo subscription, including discounts on taxi rides with Citymobil. 14 people received NFT-cards. In total the project page was visited by more than 100,000 unique users.
